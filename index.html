OUR STORY

Building Nepal's future with precision and integrity

Founded by Er. Tapendra Baduwal in 2018, Tapendra Design & Construction has grown into one of Nepal's most respected civil engineering consultancies. With deep roots in practical site knowledge and advanced technical expertise, we deliver solutions that are not only structurally sound but also economically viable and environmentally responsible.

Our team combines 6+ years of hands-on experience across residential, commercial, mixed-use, and infrastructure projects. Every project receives the same level of dedication — from a single-family home to large-scale institutional developments.
