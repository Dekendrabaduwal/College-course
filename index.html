About The Firm

Tapendra Design & Construction is a premier civil engineering consultancy based in Nepal, dedicated to delivering robust, safe, and cost-effective engineering solutions. We bridge the gap between architectural vision and structural reality.

Founded and led by Tapendra Baduwal, a dedicated professional with over 6 years of rigorous site and design experience. Our firm operates on the principles of professional ethics, technical mastery, and uncompromising quality.

Whether it is a private residence, a commercial complex, or institutional infrastructure, we ensure every blueprint translates into a lasting legacy.
